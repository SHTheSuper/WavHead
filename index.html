WavHead
Musical Democracy
Discussions are better than playlists.
WavHead is a system that lets the people at your events vote on what music to hear, from any device. Simply start it up and let it play songs in vote order, so you can get back to what's important.
Stop, collaborate, and listen.
The world's most popular language is music, start a conversation.
Any time. On any device.
Make your voice heard, whether you're on your phone, tablet, or desktop. The only requirement is a web browser.
Free and open source.
WavHead is available under the BSD license, so anyone can contribute and make it even better.
Easy to install.
Clone the repository, install TagLib (which is available through all major Linux package managers and Homebrew), index your music, and go. Detailed instructions in the README.
